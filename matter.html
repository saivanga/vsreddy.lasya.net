    • Vanga Sambi Reddy was born on June 05th 1952. In Dakshina Bharat Hindi Prachar Sabha he finished all exams. He got an admission in Hindi Teacher Training but they couldn’t afford the money so he stopped studying and started farming.

    Vanga Sambi Reddy was also a good swimmer. He was born in Munnangi village in Guntur district. In 1968, they moved to Pakalapadu village. He farmed for 3 years.

    In the 1st year, he planted rice. In the 2nd year, he planted cotton. In the 3rd year, he planted Tamarind. He married Siva Leela in 1973.

    On October 21st 1975, his elder son, Chakradhara Reddy was born. He continued farming until his second son, Sasidhar was born on March 19th 1980. In 1975, he stopped farming and joined the biggest printing press in both Guntur and Prakasam districts.

    The printing press was called Sri Lakshmi Press. Initially, he started his career in the printing field as a proofreader, composer and then moved to Desktop publishing. He initially started with Vision Tech software on an apple computer. Then, he started using Aldus PageMaker. Later, Adobe company bought Aldus PageMaker.

    So he started using Adobe’s PageMaker. He also learned CorelDraw for Vector Drawings. He worked in Sri Lakshmi Press for 20 years and later, he left the Sri Lakshmi Press to start a training institute to teach Desktop Publishing in Guntur.
